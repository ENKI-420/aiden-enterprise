    Baptist Health Medical Group Hematology & Oncology is a community based team practice of board certified specialists in adult hematology and medical oncology.
    

    We strive to provide comprehensive high quality analysis and management of blood disorders and cancer by maintaining state of the art treatment practices through affiliation with national cancer treatment study groups.
    

    Our physicians and staff are dedicated to giving our patients the best possible care in an honest, compassionate, and courteous manner.
